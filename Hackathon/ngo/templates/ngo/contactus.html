Hunger is something no one should struggle with- or be judged for.

There is a surplus amount of food and every day billions of tonnes of food get wasted but still, there are millions of people across the world who are dying of hunger. We, at DaanData aim to ensure that there should be equal distribution of food among all human beings and that each person is entitled to their fair share of food. We do so by connecting nearby Event Organisers to NGOs so that they can donate the surplus amount of food to the needy people.
